Signs Someone Secretly Loves You

Wondering if someone has feelings for you? Look out for these signs:

  • Quality Time: They make an effort to spend time with you.
  • Future Plans: You're included in their future.
  • Jealousy: They show signs of jealousy.
  • Support: They're always there for you.
  • Attention to Details: They remember little things about you.
  • Eye Contact: They often look at you.
  • Emotional Tuning: They're attuned to your feelings.
  • Privacy: They keep their romantic life private.
  • Vulnerability: They're vulnerable around you.
  • Impress: They try to impress you.
  • Protectiveness: They don't think anyone is good enough for you.
  • Distance: They distance themselves when you're in a relationship.
  • Hints: They hint at their feelings.
  • Happiness: They try to make you happy.
  • Teasing: Their friends tease them about you.
